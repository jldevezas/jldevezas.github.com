Introduction

My name is José Devezas and I am a Master in Informatics and Computing Engineering. For the last few years, I've been a researcher in social network analysis and recommender systems, but I'm currently moving into the industry, where I hope to work as data scientist and a developer in innovative projects.

I have begun my research career at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto, where I graduated and where I worked as a researcher for Laboratório SAPO/U.Porto. I was also a member of the Breadcrumbs team, as part of CRACS/INESC TEC, at the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto.

I have dedicated most of my time to the study of community detection methodologies and the analysis of communities, but I have also done work on data mining, network analysis, information retrieval and data visualization. More recently, I have tackled problems in the areas of music information retrieval, recommendation and discovery, doing applied research at Laboratório SAPO/U.Porto, as well as pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto. Some of this work has become available in this website in the form of studies, theory reviews and development tools.
